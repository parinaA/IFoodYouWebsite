Testimonials

Hardik
RATED 4.5

It was 2 in the morning. My friends were at my place and we were hungry. So we started looking for some place to deliver food and we found this amazing place in Pitampura. They took about 45 minutes to deliver but it was otally worth it. The packing is amazing and the best part is that the food is freshly prepared. I didn';t expect it to be that good.
We ordered 2 egg paranthas, one plate rajma chawal and one chicken seekh roll. All the 3 items were delicious, especially the chicken seekh roll was totally on point. Just the egg paranthas were a little dry, they should serve something with it other than the green chutney. Otherwise the food was amazing and you cannot expect anything better at 2am.

Kritesh Taneja
RATED 4.5

I was going through zomato for something easy on the pocket which also calms mad cravings and I found this treasure of a place. It only delivers food and that too at the most precious time and the food came hot and nicely packed.
Coming to the food now, I oredered non-veg club sandwich and man that was one enormous sandwich. It was so filling it had mashed chicken,potatoes,veggies and mayo. I was already so full but I had this beautiful chicken tikka pasta to deal with and it felt like I am eating italian and punjabi cuisine all together, it was a really good combination and executed very well.
I was totally amazed by the quantity and the quality of he food they provide and I only spent 300 bucks and on the top I got 15% discount on my order.
This place is a gem and so underrated.



our story

It was 3 in the morning and just one of those nights when your hunger pangs get the better of you. We were searching about frantically for a place that would get us food at that hour. The only places we could find appereared to be too shady. We realized we would not have had to face this problem if we were living in Gurgaon or South Delhi.

We thought why should Gurgaon have all the fun and that's when the idea for our venture struck us. I Food You is a venture by us to fill the void of food delivery services in the North West Delhi circuit during the night. We wanted to provide a great option to people who had late night cravings but wanted good food at the same time.
